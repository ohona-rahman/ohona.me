Overview

Dream Charter Schools needed a user-friendly and informative website to amplify their mission of supporting public charter schools. As part of the Hack4Impact team, I'm designing a platform that clearly communicates their impact and makes it easy for schools, volunteers, and donors to connect.

ðŸŽ¯ The Challenge

Dream Schools lacked an online presence, making it difficult for potential partners to discover their services. They needed a website with clear branding, easy navigation, and compelling content to attract schools in high-need communities.

Our Solution

Working collaboratively with developers and fellow designers, we're creating:

  • Mission-Driven Homepage: Clear messaging that communicates Dream Schools' impact and values
  • Service Highlights: Sections showcasing past projects, available services, and success stories
  • Engagement Tools: Contact forms for school leaders and volunteers to get involved
  • Social Media Integration: Basic profiles to amplify outreach and community engagement

Design Approach

I focused on creating an accessible, welcoming design that prioritizes clarity and trust. The visual identity uses warm colors and friendly typography to make the organization feel approachable while maintaining professionalism.

Expected Impact

Once launched, the website will:

  • Significantly increase visibility for Dream Schools among charter school leaders
  • Streamline volunteer engagement and donor discovery
  • Better serve public charter schools in high-need communities
  • Provide a scalable foundation for future digital initiatives
